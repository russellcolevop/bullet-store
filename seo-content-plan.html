A full quarter of pillar articles, scheduled week-by-week, designed to rank, convert, and compound.

One pillar article per week for 12 weeks. Each piece targets a specific long-tail keyword with commercial or informational intent, links back to relevant kit pages, and is designed to collect organic traffic that compounds month after month. By the end of Quarter 1, we have 12 ranked assets doing work 24/7 with zero recurring cost.

Quarter 2 — Preview

Q2 Themes (High Level)

Quarter 2 builds on Q1's momentum with deeper niche content, seasonal pushes, and the first round of refresh on top-performing articles. Full Q2 schedule will be drafted once we see which Q1 articles pull ahead.

  • Brand deep-dives (Knipex, Fluke, Klein, Milwaukee, DeWalt full brand guides)
  • Trade-specific guides (welder's tool kit, HVAC starter tools, plumber additions)
  • Season content (back-to-school for apprentice programs, Q4 gift guides)
  • Customer spotlights and testimonials from Q1 buyers
  • First refresh pass on Q1 articles that hit top-10 rankings
How this connects to revenue: Based on comparable trade-vertical SEO programs, expect 0 traffic in months 1–2, early rankings in month 3, and meaningful organic traffic by month 4–5. By month 9, SEO should be contributing 20–30% of total orders at a CAC well under $20. Every article stays live forever — this is the only channel where the work you do in Q1 is still paying you in Year 3.

Distribution & Amplification

Writing the article is half the job. Each piece also gets:

  • Internal linking from 3–5 relevant existing pages
  • Social media push (LinkedIn, Facebook trade groups, Reddit r/Millwrights where appropriate)
  • Email newsletter feature to the subscriber list
  • Pitched to 1–2 relevant trade publications for reprint or backlink
  • Schema markup and Open Graph tags for rich search results
