Google Ads Campaign

Paid acquisition • Phase 2 launch

What We'll Do

Stand up a Google Ads account for Bullet, build three campaign types (branded defensive, category search, Shopping for pro-tier kits), write ad copy, connect conversion tracking, and manage day-to-day optimization.

Campaign Structure

  • Branded search: Defensive bids on "Bullet Trade Services" and variants. ~$150/mo.
  • Category search: "millwright tool kit," "electrician apprentice tools," "trade starter kit." ~$1,200/mo.
  • Google Shopping: Product feed sync from WooCommerce, shopping ads on Pro-tier kits. ~$1,500/mo.

Email Marketing & Lifecycle

Owned channel • Launches Month 1

What We'll Do

Set up a Klaviyo or MailerLite account, build lifecycle automations, write evergreen campaign templates, design branded templates, and run monthly broadcasts.

The Automations

  • Welcome series (4 emails): New subscriber onboarding with trade-specific buying advice.
  • Abandoned cart (3 emails): 1hr, 24hr, 72hr sequences. Typically recovers 8–12% of carts.
  • Post-purchase series (5 emails): Shipping, care tips, cross-sell, review request, referral ask.
  • Apprentice year upgrade: 12 months after Starter kit purchase, trigger Pro kit campaign.
  • Win-back: 90-day and 180-day re-engagement flows.
