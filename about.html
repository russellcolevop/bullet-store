Our Story

Bullet Trade Services was founded in 2008 as an industrial millwrighting and electrical services company. Today, we're one of the largest industrial contractors in Southern Ontario — with over 100 employees, a 10,000 square foot facility in Puslinch, and hundreds of factory clients across the region.

We've spent nearly two decades equipping our own tradespeople with the right tools. We know what works, what breaks, what's worth the money and what isn't. When Ontario colleges started asking us to build tool kits for their trades students, we realized something: the knowledge our team carries every day is valuable beyond our own shop floor.

That's why we launched our tool kit line — to give every new tradesperson the same starting advantage our team has. Every kit is curated by our active millwrights and electricians, not by marketers looking at a spreadsheet.
